About

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Data Science and Big Data Technology at Taiyuan University of Technology, School of Artificial Intelligence.

I have been involved in projects focused on large language model development integrated with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), along with backend system development based on the Django framework.

I have received the University-Level Scholarship twice, been recognized as an Outstanding Student for Academic Excellence twice, awarded Outstanding Individual in Innovation and Entrepreneurship once, and honored as an Outstanding Individual in Academic Research once.
